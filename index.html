About Me

I’ve always been a curious and social person who loves meeting people from different backgrounds and learning about their perspectives. This curiosity about the world, combined with a desire to challenge myself, led me to study overseas. That experience not only broadened my worldview but also helped me grow personally and professionally. My interest in problem-solving naturally drew me to Computer Science. I’ve always enjoyed figuring out how things work and finding creative ways to make them better. Along the way, I discovered that development is as much about collaboration as it is about coding. Working with others has taught me how to listen, adapt, and step up when faced with challenges. As a developer, I’m driven by the opportunity to explore new ideas and technologies while working on projects that make a tangible impact. I see every new challenge as a chance to learn and grow, and I look forward to building meaningful solutions alongside others who share the same passion for creating something great.
